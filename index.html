About

Hi, I’m Haneul Yoo, an Ph.D. student advised by Alice Oh at Users & Information Lab, School of Computing, KAIST. My research interest primarily lies in the intersection of natural language processing and computational social science. In particular, I mainly work on (1) understanding historical documents in low-resource languages and (2) applying and utilizing NLP in language education. I believe there are still myriads of underrepresented languages in NLP, and our efforts on low-resource languages can build more culturally-diverse language models. I also believe language learners can contribute to NLP techniques and data and vice versa.
